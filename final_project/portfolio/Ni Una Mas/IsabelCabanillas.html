Isabel Cabanillas

Age: 26

Location: Ciudad Juárez, Mexico

Occupation: Painter, clothing designer, and women’s activist, was a member of Hijas de su Maquila Madre and volunteered with the women’s network Mesa de Mujeres.

Death: January 18, 2020. Shot to death on the sidewalk next to her bicycle. Appeared to be a targeted killing.
